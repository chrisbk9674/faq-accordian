FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes! Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium challenges. The free challenges allow you to practice HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while the premium challenges provide more advanced projects and additional resources.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! Frontend Mentor encourages users to showcase their completed projects in their portfolios. It’s a great way to demonstrate your skills to potential employers and improve your frontend development experience.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a project?

If you’re stuck, you can:

  • Review the challenge requirements carefully.
  • Search the Frontend Mentor forum for discussions on similar challenges.
  • Ask for help in the community Discord or forum.
  • Research solutions using online resources like MDN, CSS Tricks, or Stack Overflow.
  • Break down the problem and approach it step by step.
